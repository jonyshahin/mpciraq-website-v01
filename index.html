About MPC

Multi Projects Company (MPC) is a leading Iraqi firm delivering integrated solutions in investment, construction, engineering, and innovation.

Established with a vision to build Iraq’s future, MPC combines regional expertise with global standards to create projects that stand as benchmarks of quality and sustainability. From high-rise towers and residential communities to advanced smart city concepts, MPC’s legacy is defined by precision, trust, and innovation.

With certified professionals, advanced digital platforms, and international partnerships, MPC ensures every project is not only built to last – but built to shape the future.
